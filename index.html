Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a comprehensive range of construction services including commercial building construction, industrial facilities, custom home building, renovations, steel building systems, specialty installations, and design-build services. Our expertise covers everything from ground-up construction to specialized renovations for both residential and commercial clients. We handle projects of all sizes, from small residential renovations to large-scale commercial developments.

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small renovations might take a few days to weeks, while large commercial projects can span several months. Our process begins with a thorough consultation, followed by detailed planning, permits acquisition, and construction execution. We provide comprehensive project schedules during the planning phase and maintain clear communication throughout the entire process. Our project managers ensure efficient workflow and minimal disruption to your operations.

We provide comprehensive warranties on all our construction work. This includes structural warranties on new construction, workmanship guarantees on all installations and renovations, and material warranties from manufacturers. Our commercial projects come with detailed warranty documentation covering all aspects of construction. We also offer extended warranty options and maintenance programs to protect your investment long-term. All our work meets or exceeds local building codes and industry standards.

Headquartered in Randolph, MN, we serve the entire Minnesota region, including the Twin Cities metropolitan area and surrounding communities. We handle projects throughout the state for both commercial and residential clients. Our team is fully equipped to manage projects in urban, suburban, and rural locations. For large-scale commercial projects, we're willing to travel beyond our standard service area. Contact us at 612-430-7670 to discuss your project location.

We offer comprehensive, free consultations and detailed project estimates. Our estimating process includes thorough site evaluations, detailed material and labor calculations, and consideration of all project specifications. For commercial projects, we provide complete bid packages including timeline projections, material specifications, and detailed cost breakdowns. We believe in transparent pricing and will work with you to optimize your project's budget while maintaining quality standards. Contact us at 612-430-7670 to schedule a consultation.
