About Simon Construction

Building Excellence Since 2003

Simon Construction Inc. has grown from a small family business into one of Minnesota's most trusted full-service construction companies. Based in Randolph, MN, we've built our reputation on quality craftsmanship, integrity, and dedication to excellence.

As a comprehensive construction company, we handle everything from ground-up commercial buildings to residential developments, renovations, and specialized construction projects. Our expertise spans across:

  • Commercial Construction
  • Custom Home Building
  • Industrial Facilities
  • Steel Building Systems
  • Project Management
  • Design-Build Services
  • Renovation & Remodeling
  • Specialty Installations
