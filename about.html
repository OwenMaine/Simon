About Simon Construction

Based in Randolph, MN, Simon Construction Inc. has established itself as a leading provider of comprehensive construction services. We specialize in window and glass installations, combining expertise with superior craftsmanship to deliver exceptional results for both residential and commercial projects.

Our extensive service portfolio includes building design, commercial construction, home building, and specialized installations. With our experienced team and commitment to quality, we ensure every project meets the highest standards of excellence. From steel building construction to window installations, we bring professional precision to every job.
